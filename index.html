Pro Tips

Get better results.

The prompt is only part of it. Your reference photo quality matters just as much.

01

Use multiple reference photos

3 clear reference photos gives the AI more angles to work with, dramatically improving facial accuracy across all outputs.

02

Avoid heavy filters

Instagram-filtered selfies confuse the AI about your actual skin tone, face shape, and features. Use natural, unfiltered photos.

03

Good lighting in your selfie

Natural window light is ideal. Face the window, not away from it. Avoid harsh overhead lighting or backlighting.

04

Match prompt complexity to tool

Longer prompts work better in Midjourney and Nano Banana. Keep it shorter for Firefly and DALL-E 3, which handle less text.

05

Generate at least 4 variations

Don't stop at the first output. Run the same prompt 4-8 times. The variance across outputs is how you find the best result.

06

Still not happy?

Some faces are harder for AI to render accurately. If you're consistently getting results you're not satisfied with, let a professional handle it.
